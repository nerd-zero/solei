We are excited to announce the launch of Solei, a billing layer for Africa, and one of the many tools by [Nerd Zero](https://n0.rocks).

Solei is open-source payment infrastructure designed for developers building in and for Africa. It gives teams a clean, reliable way to charge customers, collect payments, and reconcile transactions — without stitching together a tangle of legacy systems that were never built for how commerce actually works here.

## The problem

Africa is one of the world's fastest-growing technology markets. Developers are shipping products, founders are building companies, and businesses are scaling across borders. But the billing infrastructure hasn't kept up.

Cross-border payments are fragmented. Invoicing is manual. Reconciliation is painful. Too many African businesses are losing time — and revenue — just trying to get paid.

The tools exist elsewhere. They just were not built for Africa.

## Enter Solei

Solei changes that. Built as a self-hostable billing layer, it gives developers:

- **Invoicing** — create and send professional invoices in minutes
- **Payment collection** — connect local and international payment methods including mobile money and card processors common across the continent
- **Reconciliation** — match payments to invoices automatically, so your books stay clean
- **Multi-currency support** — handle USD, ZWG, and more without the operational headache
- **Developer-first API** — integrate in hours, not weeks

We designed Solei to feel fast, transparent, and predictable — the way modern infrastructure should feel.

## Built by Nerd Zero

Solei is one of many tools being built at [Nerd Zero](https://n0.rocks).

We are a group of tech enthusiasts dedicated to exploring and building disruptive technologies that defy conventional norms.

That means we don't accept the premise that Africa has to wait for the rest of the world to catch up. We build the tools ourselves — solving real developer problems, rooted in African business realities, designed to help founders ship faster and operate better.

## What we believe

Our work is shaped by a few core convictions:

- **Open source first.** Infrastructure should be transparent and community-owned. Solei is open source so you can inspect it, self-host it, and contribute to it.
- **Built for reliability.** African businesses deserve tools that don't fail. We obsess over uptime, correctness, and predictability.
- **African-first design.** We build for the payment methods, currencies, and regulatory contexts that matter here — not as an afterthought.
- **Developers deserve better DX.** Great tooling should be a joy to integrate. We care deeply about clean APIs and honest documentation.
- **Putting Africa on the latest tech.** We believe African builders should have access to the same quality of infrastructure as anyone else in the world. That's what we're here to build.

## What's next

Solei is just getting started. We're building more integrations, expanding currency support, and developing the features that African businesses have told us they need most.

If you are building for Africa, or building from Africa, we'd love to hear from you. Try Solei, open an issue, or reach out directly — we are a small team and we read everything.

We're just getting started.
